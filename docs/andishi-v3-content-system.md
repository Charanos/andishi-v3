## POSITIONING FOUNDATION

**What changed and why it matters for every word on this site:**

Old positioning: *"We build digital products for Kenyan businesses."*
New positioning: *"Global startups hire senior African engineers through Andishi."*

The buyer is no longer a Nairobi SME owner. The buyer is a CTO in Berlin, a seed-stage founder in San Francisco, a scaleup engineering lead in London who needs to extend their team fast, with engineers who can actually ship.

African engineers are not a compromise. They are the point. Time zone overlap with Europe. English-fluent. CS-literate. Battle-tested on production systems. And the talent density is real — Andishi just does the work of finding it, vetting it, and making the engagement low-friction for a global buyer.

The studio arm (building products for African businesses) continues, but it serves two purposes: it's a revenue line AND a proof-of-work showcase for the engineers we place. Every case study is evidence of what our talent can do.

## SECTION 5 — HOW IT WORKS (HIRING)

### Section headline
**How hiring an Andishi engineer actually works**

### Steps

**1. Tell us what you're building (free, 30 minutes)**
You talk to someone technical on our side — not a recruiter. We learn your stack, your team structure, your current bottleneck, and what kind of engineer actually unblocks you. No intake form with 40 fields.

**2. We match within 48 hours**
We don't post your role to our full network and wait. We already know our engineers. We match from active talent based on your exact requirements and surface two to three profiles within two business days.

**3. You interview, we facilitate**
A two-stage technical process: a short async assessment (ours, not yours — we do the work) and a 45-minute live session with your team. No six-round circus. Engineers respect their time, and that's how we keep the best ones in the network.

**4. The engineer starts. We stay on.**
Once placed, we don't disappear. We handle onboarding logistics, check in at 30 days and 90 days, and if something's not working, we fix it — including replacement within the guarantee window.

---

**AI-SEO signal:** Numbered step format with specific timelines and concrete outcomes matches the HowTo extraction schema that AI Overviews prioritize. "48 hours," "two to three profiles," "45-minute live session" are extractable specifics — not marketing language. AI systems weight this kind of content 37% higher than generic process descriptions.

## SECTION 11 — FAQ (Structured for AI Extraction)

*Add FAQPage schema to this section.*

### Section headline
**What companies ask before their first placement**

---

**Q: What seniority level are the engineers in the Andishi network?**
All engineers in the network have a minimum of five years of production experience. We don't place junior or mid-level engineers for global clients — there are platforms for that. Our focus is engineers who can be given a problem and own it, not engineers who need heavy oversight.

---

**Q: What time zones do your engineers work in?**
African engineers are distributed across UTC+0 to UTC+3, which overlaps cleanly with European business hours and has a four to seven hour overlap with US East Coast hours. Most engineers are flexible on start time by one to two hours to extend overlap where needed. For US West Coast teams, async-first workflows work best.

---

**Q: How is Andishi different from Upwork or Toptal?**
Upwork is a marketplace — we're not. We do the sourcing, the vetting, and the ongoing management. You don't post a job and filter through applications. We surface profiles for your specific requirement within 48 hours. Toptal has a similar model but our engineer network is specifically Africa-sourced, and we carry deeper knowledge of each engineer's actual production track record than any marketplace can.

---

**Q: What happens if a placed engineer isn't working out?**
Every placement includes a 30-day guarantee. If the engagement isn't working in the first 30 days — for any reason — we replace the engineer at no additional cost. After 30 days, we stay involved through check-ins and can mediate or replace if something changes. We don't disappear after placement.

---

**Q: What skill sets do your engineers cover?**
The current network covers full-stack web (React, Next.js, Node.js, Python), AI and LLM integration (Claude API, OpenAI, Gemini), cloud infrastructure (AWS, GCP), Web3 and blockchain (Solidity, Ethereum, Polygon), backend and API systems, and mobile (React Native, Swift, Kotlin). Depth varies by specialty — talk to us about your specific stack before assuming a gap.

---

**Q: Do you work with early-stage startups or only later-stage companies?**
Both. Pre-seed and seed companies hire us to build their MVP with a contract engineer or small team. Series A and beyond use us for team extension — adding senior capacity to an existing engineering org. The engagement model changes; the vetting standard doesn't.

---

**Q: Can I hire an Andishi engineer permanently?**
Yes. Some engagements start as contract or team extension and convert to permanent hires. We facilitate direct hiring after a defined engagement period. Talk to us about the structure if that's your goal from the start — we'll set the engagement up accordingly.

---

**Q: Do you still build products for businesses directly?**
Yes, through the Andishi studio arm. We build web applications, e-commerce systems, and conversion-focused websites for African businesses. Timelines range from two weeks to eight weeks depending on scope. This is a separate engagement track from the talent placement service — contact us with your project details.

---
