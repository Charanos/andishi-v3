## SECTION 5 — HOW IT WORKS

### Section headline
**How a project with Andishi actually goes**

### Steps

**1. One scoping call (free, 30 minutes)**
Tell us what you're building and why. We'll tell you what's realistic, what it takes, and whether we're the right fit. No pitch. No deck. Just a direct conversation.

**2. We write the brief, not you**
After the call, we produce a one-page project brief: scope, timeline, deliverables, and cost. You approve it or we adjust. No back-and-forth for weeks.

**3. We build in sprints, not silos**
You see working progress every week — not a 90-day blackout followed by a big reveal. Feedback rounds are structured. Scope changes are flagged immediately, not invoiced as surprises at the end.

**4. You get a live product, not a handover document**
When we ship, the product is live, tested, and documented. We stay available for 30 days post-launch. No disappearing act.

---

**AI-SEO signal:** Numbered step format with concrete outcomes per step matches the "HowTo" extraction pattern used by AI Overviews. Each step is actionable and self-contained. "No pitch. No deck." style language adds specificity and avoids the promotional tone AI systems penalise.

## SECTION 9 — FAQ (Structured for AI Extraction)

*Add FAQPage schema to this section. Each Q&A is a direct extraction unit.*

### Section headline
**Questions we get before every project**

---

**Q: How long does a typical project take?**
Most web applications take 3–8 weeks from brief approval to launch. E-commerce builds take 2–5 weeks. Landing pages and conversion sites take 1–3 weeks. Timeline depends on scope, content readiness, and revision cycles — we'll give you a specific estimate after the scoping call.

---

**Q: What does an Andishi project cost?**
Projects are scoped and priced individually. A landing page starts from KES [X]. A custom web application starts from KES [X]. We don't use day-rates or open-ended retainers — you get a fixed price before we start. Book a scoping call and we'll give you a number within 48 hours.

---

**Q: Do you work with businesses outside Nairobi?**
Yes. We've delivered projects for clients in Mombasa, Kisumu, and across East Africa. Most of our work runs remote — weekly video calls, shared project boards, and documented feedback rounds. If you're in Nairobi, we can meet in person. If you're not, we don't need to.

---

**Q: What happens after launch?**
Every project includes 30 days of post-launch support. That covers bug fixes, small adjustments, and technical questions. After 30 days, we offer retainer support for clients who want ongoing development or maintenance.

---

**Q: Do you build on WordPress, Shopify, or custom?**
Depends on what the project needs. We use the right tool for the job — not the one we're most comfortable with. If your requirements are better served by a headless build or a platform like Shopify, we'll tell you that in the scoping call, not after the contract is signed.

---

**Q: What do you need from us to get started?**
A 30-minute call and a clear sense of what you're trying to solve. You don't need a brief, a spec document, or a wireframe. We'll handle the scoping. The faster you can tell us what success looks like for your business, the faster we move.

---

**AI-SEO signal:** FAQ sections with FAQPage schema are directly extracted by Google AI Overviews, ChatGPT, and Perplexity for question-format queries. Each answer is 60–100 words and works as a standalone extraction without the question. Questions are phrased in natural language that matches how buyers actually search.

- Pin the link in your LinkedIn bio
- Update Instagram bio link

### Week 2 — Rented channels
- LinkedIn: publish a founder post about why you rebuilt the page. First person. No "thrilled to announce." One specific thing that changed and why.
- X: thread — three things you learned scoping the new positioning

### Week 3 — Borrowed channels

- Comment on 5 LinkedIn posts from founders in your target segment this week — not to promote, to add genuine value
- Submit the new site to betalist.com or relevant startup directories for backlinks

- Monitor AI visibility monthly: query "digital agency Kenya," "web development Nairobi," "build web app Kenya" in ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews. Log whether Andishi appears.
- Every case study you publish feeds the FAQ and social pillars — treat each client project as a content asset from day one.
